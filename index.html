Optimized transaction fees

Our service allows you to bundle multiple payments into a single transaction to lower the blockchain mining fees and increase processing and confirmations speed.

High-grade information security.

To keep your funds secure, we have implemented an improved multisig algorithm. Our keys are stored and encrypted separately on different servers.

Custom low-cost Ethereum MultiSig

We have implemented custom multisig for all Ethereum transactions sent from our wallets. This substantially improves your security without imposing costs of generating new contracts.

High level of fault tolerance and load

Our server infrastructure consists of multiple load-balanced clusters around the world.Together with Cloudflare we deliver protection from DDOS attacks and 99.9% uptime, so you can never lose a client or miss any payments.

All in One

Convenient management of multiple wallets on your business account page. Single sign-on and verification make running several businesses easy and simple.
